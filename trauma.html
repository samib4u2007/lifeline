Trauma Care

When a patitent is discharged from hospital after a prolonged treatment for trauma, care and attention are needed to prevent complications like:


A. Bleeding:- Improper management by unskilled individuals can cause bleed through treated wounds.
B. Infections:- Improper dressings/not maintaining aseptic conditions will cause infection.
C. Sepsis:- This might begin as urinarytract infection, Pneumonia, wound, Pressure ulcer (or) other infection in bed reidden patients. This condition may lead to shock, multiple organ failure and death specially if not recognized early and treated properly.
D. varitions in vitals:- Fluctuations in BP, Pulse, Hypotension may be life threatning.
E. Improper Dressing.
