Care for Paralyzed Patients

Care for Paralyzed Patients should be in the form of

a. Body care:- Maintaining good hygene, grooming, providing clean bed.
b. Medication:- Providing prescribed medication in time is most important to treat underling cause.
c. Nutritional care:- Taking care of diet & appetite.
d. Emotional care:- Depression is very common in paralyzed person. This causes changes in weight, over sleeping mood changes etc.

Complications of paralysis:

If proper care is not given following complications may occur.

1. Bladder and Bowel Management:-Paralysis at any level causes Poor/No bladder & Bowel control, as a result patient urinates/defecates in bed unintentionally, this makes him to feal guilty and separated from his own loved ones. If proper attention is not payed urinary tract infections will develop this further worsen the condition.

2. Bed sores:- Another yet most painful conditions of bed ridden patient is Bed sores. Which are painful if not managed in a proper way will delay the recovery of patient.

Bed sores are uclers of skin & underlying tissues resulting from prolanged pressure.
* People most at risk of bed sores are bed ridden patients.
* Symptoms -> unusual changes in skin color and texture.
* Swelling.
* Pus like draining.
* Pain & tenderness.
* Special care is require to treat bedsores.
3. Skin Care:- In Paralyzed patients, hygene is compromized, there are more chances of Skin Infections.

4. Sepsis:- This might begin as urinarytract infection, Pneumonia, wound, Pressure ulcer (or) other infection in bed reidden patients. This condition may lead to shock, multiple organ failure and death specially if not recognized early and treated properly.
