Non Healing Wounds

Common non healing wounds are:-


A. Diabetic foot.
B. Venous leg ulcers.
C. Arterial insufficency.
D. Pressure ulcers.

These are the common wounds which require special care by trained individuals. These wounds require proper debridement compression stocking and bandage.

If all these are not done properly can lead to:
A. Infections.
B. Osteomyelitis.
C. Tissue necrosis and gangrine etc...
