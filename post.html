Post Operative Care For Fractures:

*Post operative care is as important as surgical treatment for fractures.
*Broken bones takes around 2-3 months to heal(to form a temporary bone). This temporary bone is not as strong as real bone and slowly gets replaced by strong bone which takes another 2-3 months.
*If proper care and attention are not payed most possible complications which can occur are:
1.Poor alignment of bone.
2.Infection.
3.Improper practice of rehabilitation techniques, improper usage of crutches further worsen the condition and leads to mal-union.
4.Improper monitoring of skin under cast will make the skin itchy and senstive to infection.
5.Lack of regular inspection can cause swelling, blueness or loss of movement of fingers/toes, numbness or increased pain.
6.As Fractured area is immobilized for a period of 3-4 months, surroundings muscles becomes weak, if proper care is not given rehabilitation becomes difficult.
