Oldage Care

Taking care and fullfillment of specical needs and requirements which are unique for elders has become a challenge for younger generation due to busy schedules.

Your loved ones really need some assistance with daily activities and health care. many of you will put all your efforts to fullfill their requirements but may find them unsatisfied because they need your Quality Time.

Basic oldage care includes
* Nursing care procedures
* Restorative care
* Safe behavioural management in dementia care.
* Health emergency responses, related to falls and infections compounded by co-morbidties.
* Pallative care including complex pain management.
* Medication administration and management consistent with quality use of medicines guidelines.
* Infection prevention and control Programs.

Talking to them, going for a drive, playing board games, watching a movies, looking through the photo albums etc... will help in prevention of depression
