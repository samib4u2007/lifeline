Diabetic Foot


Diabetes-- Now-a-days this is more common disease due to our diet and less active life style.

Prolonged periods of high blood sugar levels can damage many areas of body including feet. Diabetes is responsible for over 50% of foot amputations in india.

If a person does not receive treatment for infection, ulcers can develop and may lead to gangrene formation.

If care is not priovided doctor might suggest amputation.
